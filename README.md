### Data Visualization and Storytelling

#### Objective
The main goal of this project was to:
1. Create clear and meaningful visualizations
2. Choose the right charts for different types of data
3. Highlight key business insights
4. Avoid clutter and keep the dashboard clean and easy to understand

#### Tools Used
1. Tableau / Power BI
2. Superstore Sales Dataset

#### What I Built
1. Sales by Category: I used a bar chart to compare sales across different categories to identify which category performs the best.
2. Profit by Region: This visualization shows how profit varies across regions, making it easy to see which areas are more profitable.
3. Sales Over Month: A line chart was created to understand how sales change over time and to identify growth patterns.
4. Top Customers: This chart highlights the top 10 customers who contribute the most to sales.
5. Profit vs Sales: A scatter plot was used to explore the relationship between sales and profit.

#### Key Insights
1. The Technology category generates the highest sales
2. Some regions perform better in terms of profit
3. Sales show an overall increasing trend
4. A small number of customers contribute a large portion of revenue
5. High sales do not always mean high profit

This project showed me how powerful data visualization can be when done correctly. 
A well-designed dashboard not only looks good but also helps in understanding the data better and making informed decisions.
